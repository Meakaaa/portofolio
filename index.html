Hello I'm Nurrudin Wiranda!

Who am I?

I'm Nurrudin Wiranda, M.Cs

I was born in Palangkaraya on March 15, 1990. I studied S1 (S.Kom) at STMIK Amikom Yogyakarta and earned a Master of Computer Science (M.Cs) degree from the Department of Computer Science, UGM, Yogyakarta in 2016. My main research interests are Machine Learning, Mobile Computing and Embedded Systems. I am active as a researcher and lecturer, where many research publications in the form of scientific papers and articles have been published in various conference proceedings, scientific journals, and portals, both nationally and internationally.

I have experience of winning several research grants; holds some software copyrights; published a number of books and articles in Indonesian and international papers in the form of proceedings and journals. I am a professional member of scientific associations IEEE Computer Society (97956108), INDOCEISS (2201-32000451), and APTIKOM (AP-22.00015). I am also a founder of Birran Cipta Informatika (birran.com), a company engaged in software development, enterprise architecture, professional training and certification center.

I have experience as a software developer in various government and private institutions, such as PDAM Kandangan, PT PLN UPDK Barito, PT PLN UPT KalselTeng, PT PJBS East Java, PT PJBS Kuala Kapuas, IAIN Palangkaraya, Universitas Proklamasi 45 Yogyakarta, etc
